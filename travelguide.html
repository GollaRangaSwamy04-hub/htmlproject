Welcome to Goa

Goa is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, churches, nightlife, seafood, and water sports.

Top Tourist Places

  • Baga Beach
  • Calangute Beach
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  • Fort Aguada
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus

Best Time to Visit

November to February

Popular Foods

  1. Fish Curry Rice
  2. Prawn Curry
  3. Bebinca
  4. Goan Vindaloo

Travel Tips

✔ Carry sunscreen.
✔ Wear comfortable clothes.
✔ Stay hydrated.
✔ Follow local rules and keep the beaches clean.
